# ChainGrant - Project Details

## Overview
ChainGrant is a blockchain-based grant and fund tracking system designed to bring transparency and accountability to milestone-based disbursements. It leverages the Algorand blockchain (simulated in this version) to ensure that funds are only released when specific, verifiable goals are met.

## Key Roles

### 1. Sponsor
- **Dashboard**: View high-level stats (Total Grants, Funds Locked, Pending Milestones).
- **Create Grant**: Define project goals, total funding (₹), and a detailed milestone roadmap.
- **Co-Sponsorship**: Ability to add multiple sponsors to a single grant for pooled funding.
- **Milestone Approval**: Review student-submitted proof and release funds securely.

### 2. Student
- **Dashboard**: Track active grants, completed milestones, and earned reputation NFTs.
- **Milestone Submission**: Upload proof of work (generates SHA-256 hash) for sponsor review.
- **Proof of Spend**: Upload receipts for expenses, creating an immutable audit trail on-chain.
- **Reputation Building**: Earn NFT badges for successful project completions.

## Core Features

### 1. Milestone-Based Funding
Funds are locked in a smart contract and released incrementally as milestones are completed and approved.

### 2. On-Chain Transparency
- **Transparency Wall**: A public audit page showing all grants, sponsors, and transaction timelines.
- **Proof of Work**: Every milestone completion is backed by a cryptographic hash of the submitted proof.
- **Proof of Spend**: Spending receipts are hashed and "stored" on-chain to prevent tampering.

### 3. Reputation System (NFT Badges)
Students earn non-transferable (soulbound) NFT badges upon completing projects, building a verifiable Web3 resume.
